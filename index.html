PROJECT OVERVIEW

DeepGaming will be a neural network based AI that we can train to play board games on BoardGameArena website.

Reinforcement learning is one of the most exciting areas of data science, yet one which we barely covered in the MIDS program. Also, while there have been many similar board game AI projects in the past few years, DeepGaming will be the first reinforcement learning AI to train and operate on a publicly accessible gaming website.

Goals

  • Build end to end product
  • Learn about state-of-the-art reinforcement learning techniques
  • Lay groundwork for generic AI framework

Impact

  • This is fun!
  • Self education
  • Extension can be applied to any other game on site

MODEL

Q Learning Approach

  • AI employs reinforcement learning algorithm along with neural networks
  • Feature engineering of board game state to reduce noise and improve training
  • Reduction of number of states in the action space to general moves such as attack and defend to optimize performance and training
  • Self play algorithm allows AI to play with itself to improve artificial intelligence
